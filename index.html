About Sri Pranavi Chintalapudi

Her journey in Kuchipudi and beyond

Sri Pranavi Chintalapudi began her Kuchipudi training at the age of 8 under the guidance of Smt. Preethi Tatambhotla. Over the past 11 years, she has performed at esteemed venues like Nadasneerajanam (Tirumala), Guruvayurappa Temple (Kerala), and Chidambaram Temple (Tamil Nadu).

A versatile artist, she has been learning Carnatic vocal music since age 5 under Smt. Gayatri Sriram and Smt. Poorima Rao, and is currently under Smt. Prema Iyer. She also studies Carnatic violin and Sanskrit, and is pursuing an engineering degree at BITS Pilani, Dubai Campus.

She is also pursuing a Diploma at the University of Silicon Andhra.

Meet Guru Srimati Preethi Tatambhotla

Guiding Pranavi's artistic journey

Srimati Preethi Tatambhotla is a revered exponent of Kuchipudi, dedicated to preserving and propagating this exquisite classical dance form. She began her profound journey in Kuchipudi at a tender age under the tutelage of Guru Padmasri Dr. Sobha Naidu garu, a direct disciple of the legendary Guru Dr.Vempati Chinna Satyam garu.

With over **[Confirm her actual years of experience, e.g., 25]** years of experience as a performer and a passionate educator, Srimati Preethi has graced numerous stages across India and the Middle East. Her performances are celebrated for their emotive depth, intricate footwork, and captivating storytelling.

As the founder and artistic director of Tanmayi Art Studio, she has mentored countless students, instilling in them not just the technical prowess of Kuchipudi but also a deep appreciation for its cultural and spiritual essence.

Her teaching methodology emphasizes discipline, devotion, and a holistic understanding of the art. Pranavi has flourished under her expert guidance for 11 years, a testament to Srimati Preethi's profound dedication and artistry.
