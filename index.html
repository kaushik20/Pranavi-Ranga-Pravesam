About Sri Pranavi Chintalapudi

Her journey in Kuchipudi and beyond

Sri Pranavi Chintalapudi is an aspiring Engineering student at BITS Pilani, Dubai Campus in Computer Science, and a dedicated Kuchipudi dancer, training under Smt. Preethi Tatambhotla for the past eight years.

She started learning Kuchipudi at an age of 5 and blossomed into an accomplished dancer under tutelage of Preethi.

A certified Kuchipudi dancer from University of Silicon Andhra, USA, currently pursuing her Diploma while aptly balancing her academics.

Pranavi has played lead roles with her Guru in ballets like Kalyana Srinivasam and Navarasa Natabhamini and won accolades from artists such as Sri Tanikella Bharani Garu and Oscar Awardee Sri Chandra Bose Garu .

A recipient of titles, “Nritya Vismaya” and “Naatya Surabhi”, she performed across India and UAE at various religious and cultural venues such as Nadaneerajanam, Guruvayoor, Chidambaram and Kolhapur.

Pranavi is a winner of several prizes which include Shoha Naidu International Award, initiated in the memory of the Guru of her Guru.

A versatile artist, a devoted rasika of music, Pranavi began learning Carnatic music at the age of five, completed the 4th grade of 8- Grade music certification offered by Bridge Academy,Chennai with an “Outstanding” remark.

An ardent lover of instruments, she earned acclaim on Carnatic Violin.

An aspiring polyglot, she is committed to mastering Sanskrit from Sanskrit Bharathi Institute LLC, Dubai.

Meet Guru Srimati Preethi Tatambhotla

Guiding Pranavi's artistic journey

Guru Smt. Preethi Tatambhotla is an accomplished Kuchipudi dancer and a devoted disciple of the legendary Padma Shri Dr. Sobha Naidu.

Her journey began at the age of seven and has grown into a lifelong dedication to the art.

She has performed across prestigious stages in India, both as a soloist and in her guru’s celebrated productions.

She is the recipient of several titles, including Nritya Mani, Nritya Vilasini, Nritya Jyothi, Satyabhama, and Saptarang Kala Bhushan.

A two-time Guinness World Record holder, a Young Artiste Scholarship awardee from the Ministry of Culture and a Doordarshan accredited artiste, her talent has received wide acclaim.

Smt. Preethi holds a Master’s degree in Kuchipudi from the University of Silicon Andhra.

Beyond performance, she curated Nrithyarchana, a UAE- based initiative celebrating classical dance.

She has had the honour of performing before dignitaries such as Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi, former Vice President Shri M. Venkaiah Naidu, and AP Chief Minister Shri N. Chandrababu Naidu.

She has been recognized by multiple organizations extensively across UAE , India and other countries for her contribution towards the world of Indian classical Dance.

With a vision to promote Kuchipudi globally, she founded Tanmayi Art Studio in the UAE, where she trains and mentors aspiring dancers.

As a performer, choreographer, and guru, Smt. Preethi Tatambhotla continues to carry forward her guru’s legacy, inspiring the next generation to shine through classical dance.
